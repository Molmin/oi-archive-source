
# Description

<div class="content"><div style="text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 120%"><span style="font-size: medium">给定n个字符串，询问每个字符串有多少子串（不包括空串）是所有n个字符串中至少k个字符串的子串？</span></div></div>

# Input

<div class="content"><div style="text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 120%"><span style="font-size: medium"><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">第一行两个整数</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">n</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">，</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">k</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">。</span></span></div>
<div style="text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 120%"><span style="font-size: medium"><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">接下来</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">n</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">行每行一个字符串</span>。</span></div></div>

# Output

<div class="content"><div style="text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 120%"><span style="font-size: medium"><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">一行</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">n</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">个整数，第</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">i</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">个整数表示第</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">i</span><span style="color: #200000; line-height: 120%">个字符串的答案</span>。</span></div></div>

# Sample Input

<div class="content"><span class="sampledata">3 1<br/>
abc<br/>
a<br/>
ab<br/>
</span></div>

# Sample Output

<div class="content"><span class="sampledata">6 1 3</span></div>

# Hint

<div class="content"><p></p><p><br/><br/>
对于 100% 的数据，1&lt;=n，k&lt;=10^5，所有字符串总长不超过10^5，字符串只包含小写字母。</p><p></p></div>
